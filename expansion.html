(i) 2a + b

(ii) 3a + 7b

(iii) 3a – 4b

(iv) 3a/2b – 2b/3a

(a + b)2 = a2 + b2 + 2ab and

(a – b)2 = a2 + b2 – 2ab

= 9a2/4b2 + 4b2/9a2 – 2

10. Prove that:

We have, x2 + y2 + z2 – xy – yz – zx

= x2 + x2 + y2 + y2 + z2 + z2 – 2xy – 2yz – 2zx

= (x2 + y2 – 2xy) + (z2 + x2 – 2zx) + (y2 + z2 – 2yz)
